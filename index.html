About Me


Greetings, I'm Gabbi! Though my full name is actually Gabriella, I prefer to go by Gabbi. I currently live in Mukilteo, Washington after moving to the area from Salt Lake City in August of last year.


At the moment, I serve as a Legal Receptionist and Administrative Assitant for a law firm in Edmonds, Washington. I am the first point of contact for all incoming phone calls and in-office client interactions. I also handle administartive duties for the firm including the creation and organization of physical and electronic files, generating reports, sorting mail, directing potential new clients, etc. As a person who likes people, I very much enjoy the interactions I experience each day at work.
